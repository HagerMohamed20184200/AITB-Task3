Farafra Oasis
The most isolated oasis in the Western Desert, Farafra is the closest oasis to the White Desert, a desert dreams cape populated with unusual chalky rock formations. A safari in the White Desert is a must-do on your Western Desert exploration trail. While in Farafra, you can also visit the unusual Badr Museum or take a short ride from the oasis' only town, Qasr Al-Farafra, to reach the Bir Sitta hot springs and the El-Mufid Lake. During your stay, you'll also have the chance to see local arts and crafts and buy items like; sweaters, socks and scarves knitted out of camel and sheep wool from locals. Temperatures in Farafra are cool from October to April and can reach 35-45 C in summer.
